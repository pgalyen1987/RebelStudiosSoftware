Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Rebel Studios Software (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates rebelstudiossoftware.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information:

  • Contact form data: If you email us through our contact form, we receive your name, email address, and message content.
  • Automatically collected data: Like most websites, our hosting provider and third-party services may collect standard log data such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: Our blog uses cookies for advertising purposes through Google AdSense (see below).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and project requests
  • Improve our website and content
  • Serve relevant advertisements on our blog
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

Google AdSense

Our blog displays advertisements served by Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC. Google and its partners may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites on the Internet.

When you submit our contact form, your message is processed by FormSubmit.co to deliver it to our email inbox. FormSubmit may temporarily process your name, email, and message content for delivery purposes.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data, see How Google uses information from sites that use its services and the Google Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external sites such as GitHub, Google Play, and client projects. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party websites.

Data Retention

Contact inquiries are retained only as long as needed to respond and manage our business relationship. Server logs are retained according to our hosting provider’s policies.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Children’s Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@rebelstudiossoftware.com.
