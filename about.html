Who We Are

Rebel Studios Software is an independent software studio founded by Patrick Galyen. We specialize in native Android development, full-stack web applications, and applied AI research — with a focus on shipping real products, not slide decks.

Every project in our portfolio is something we built and deployed: apps live on Google Play, SaaS platforms in production, and open-source tools like Vigilo for on-device IoT anomaly detection.
