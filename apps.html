Phuzzles

A picture messaging app where photos are sent as scrambled puzzles. Take or choose a photo, select a difficulty (3x3 up to 6x6 grid), send it to a friend, and they must tap-to-swap the pieces to reveal and unlock the image. Full contacts system, push notifications, and solve-time stats.

  • 4 difficulty levels: Easy (3x3) to Expert (6x6)
  • Real-time puzzle status via Firestore listeners
  • Push notifications when a puzzle arrives (FCM)
  • Username-based contacts system
  • Solve time and move count tracking
  • Save solved images to device gallery
  • Firebase App Check for API security

MemorySwipe

A memory and concentration game where you watch a pattern of swipes and then repeat it in order. Each round the sequence gets longer. Compete for the highest score, track your personal bests, and improve focus and working memory all while having fun. Features AdMob integration for monetization.

ToiletTeller

A lighthearted stress-relief app where you type whatever's on your mind — frustrations, worries, or random thoughts — and flush them away with a satisfying animated swirl. A custom toilet character reacts to what you type. Sometimes the best thing you can do is just flush it and move on. Features a flush counter and interstitial ads.
